About Me

I'm Wyclef Cabana and I'm a

Musinguzi Wyclef better known by the stage name Wyclef Cabana is a Ugandan Singer, Professional Teacher, Mental Health Advocate, Song Writer, Business Man, Guitarist, Life Coach and Actor signed under ONE TYM AVENUE.
He was born on 18th October, 1998 at Nsambya Hospital, becoming the fourth from the family of six by Mr and Mrs. Kasangaki.

Music Career
Musinguzi Wyclef's musical career started way back in 2012 under the stage name Wyclef De Warren. His first single was Yenze in 2012 which was produced by Ronnie Da Don. In 2015, he released Lumonde, Kuda Kuda Dance and Obvious Case in 2016. He started out a amusical band known as Purity Band that he founded with his Primary School OBs namely; Ayine Darwin, Kamese, Ssentumbwe Eddy among others. Since gigs in the band were seasonal, he worked with a Funeral Service Company known as Curious Funeral Services located in Mengo as a pole barerto sustain himself throughout 2017 in his vacation.
He later joined Kibz Band led by Irene Kayemba, H.E Bobi Wine's elder sister as a performing artist and gigs man.
However, he rebranded from Wyclef De Warren to Wyclef Cabana.
He is currently working under various projects with the Happy African Boys under Dr. Brain, Bomba and 'party' hit maker Alien Skin.
Management Cabana is currently under the management of ONE TYM AVENUE a Kampala based enterpriseaimed at nurturing and showcasing talents of the youth to the globe

Business
As a business man, Cabana has been able to establish an ice cream business known as CABANA ICE CREAM, CABANA EXOTIC CUTS, CHARGER YO where he has got many endorsements and partnerships from different brands.
